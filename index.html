Capability Statement

My name is Sakiru Ayanyemi Ganiyu, I am a trained dental surgeon from the College of Medicine University of Lagos and a Master of Public Health graduate from Western Sydney University. I am seeking employment as a public health professional with the New South Wales (NSW) Health. I have been in the business of health care for more than two decades. I have pioneered four viable practices in the commercial capital of Nigeria-Lagos. I came into Australia to improve my skills in public health in Western Sydney University. This has enabled me to gain the needed “doxa” in the field of public health, a skill Grenfell and Wacquant showed to be transferable in their studies (Grenfell, 2009; Wacquant, 1998). Western Sydney University has greatly increased my capitals, the knowledge I gained has helped me to become a better public health administrator, I have increased my social capital with interactions with my lecturers and my colleagues who are also from diverse backgrounds (Wacquant, 1998). Thus, contributing to my social capitals (Wacquant, 1998) and has greatly improves my skills in the space of public health. Western Sydney University has also helped me to meet industry heavy weights some even taught us as guest lecturer, one of such rear privilege was Professor Foley who taught us health care financing. This exposure has enlightened me more about health economics as the bedrock for any functional health system (Green, 2007). This has helped me to understand how a functional health system works.
